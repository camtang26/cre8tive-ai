## Core USP Themes
The following differentiators reappear across every copy exercise; treat them as non-negotiable pillars.

### 1. Speed Without Sacrificing Vision
- **Value prop:** Collapse brief creation from 2–3 days (or weeks) to ~10 minutes while maintaining stakeholder control.
- **Copy anchors:** “From Brief to Storyboard in Minutes”, “96% time reduction (weeks → minutes)”, “Escape creative bottleneck hell.”
- **Evidence:** Phase 2 strategies (speed stats), transcript VP1 highlights, CTA urgency from `f398d40c`.

### 2. Hybrid AI + Human Craft
- **Value prop:** Image-to-video workflow, director-led oversight, professional-grade finishing.
- **Copy anchors:** “Image-to-Video: Quality Over Speed”, “Hybrid AI + human workflow = top 1% production values.”
- **Evidence:** `COPY_IMPLEMENTATION_GUIDE.md` (Studios hero + value cards); Studios strategy doc (craft mastery pillar).

### 3. Multi-Platform Native Delivery
- **Value prop:** No lazy crops; bespoke outputs for YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, X, Facebook with multiple duration variants.
- **Copy anchors:** “Native for Every Major Platform”, “Creates multiple durations (15s/30s/60s/90s) and specs for ad delivery.”
- **Evidence:** Multi-platform section in copy guide; Step 04 rewrite in session `84dff2c5`.

### 4. End-to-End Pipeline (Briefing Engine → Studios)
- **Value prop:** Seamless handoff from AI-generated storyboard to Cre8tive AI Studios production.
- **Copy anchors:** “Brief → Narrative → Style → Boards”, “Storyboard in minutes, Studios produces the video.”
- **Guardrail:** Per session `2de6d693`, never promise video delivery in ten minutes—keep “10-minute storyboards” framing.

### 5. Proof & Trust Signals
- **Value prop:** 4.8/5 satisfaction, 60% first-draft approval, 200+ projects, SOC2 readiness roadmap.
- **Usage:** Subtle stats line under CTA (`681b84c7`), security badges + compliance cards introduced in `84dff2c5` plan, testimonial section in copy guide.

### Workflow for Approving New Metrics
1. **Source discovery:** Analysts/marketers propose citation with link and publication date. Reject anything older than 24 months unless still authoritative.
2. **Validation review:** Analytics/strategy team verifies methodology and extracts exact phrasing permitted by source.
3. **Repository update:** Save source under `docs/insights/external/<slug>/` with `source.txt` and `summary.md`; note allowed statistics.
4. **Copy integration:** Reference this catalog when drafting; include inline comment (`<!-- citation: lemonlight-2025 -->`) to trace provenance.
5. **Quarterly audit:** Reconfirm that metrics remain current; retire or replace outdated stats during scheduled content reviews.

## Messaging Guardrails & Objection Handling
- **Accuracy First:** Do not imply video production happens in minutes; highlight Studios delivers “days to weeks depending on complexity” (`2de6d693`).
- **AI Augmentation vs Replacement:** Emphasize “Your Vision, Visualized—Instantly” to soothe fears of losing creative control (value prop #2).
- **Differentiation from commodity AI:** Reinforce hybrid image-to-video quality, director nodes, and brand-locking to distance from “prompted” competitors.
- **Competitive Urgency:** CTA/body copy should communicate that revision cycles equal lost market share (`f398d40c`).
- **Research Listings:** When referencing metrics (e.g., 60% first-draft approval, $8–13k savings), ensure ongoing analytics validation (see Phase 1 evidence workflow outputs in `docs/insights/gemini-transcript-analysis/phase1-extractions-validated/`).
